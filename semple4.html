About Our Mission

Farmers often face a tough decision: what crop will thrive in their unique soil and climate conditions? Unpredictable weather and varying seasonal factors can lead to crop failures and wasted resources. Our mission with **Harvest AI** is to provide a data-driven solution to this age-old problem.

We leverage machine learning models, historical data, and real-time environmental APIs to deliver accurate, data-backed crop recommendations. By doing so, we aim to optimize farming practices, enhance productivity, and promote sustainable agriculture.

How It Works

1. Input Your Data

Enter key details like your soil type, climate, and the current season. This takes just a few minutes!

2. We Analyze It

Our powerful AI model processes your input with real-time weather and soil data from global APIs.

3. Get Your Recommendations

Receive a tailored list of the most suitable crops, along with tips on irrigation and fertilizer use.
